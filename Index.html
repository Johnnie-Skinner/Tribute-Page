A timeline of events in the life of this great man:

  • 1874- Winston Churchill Born in Oxfordshire, England
  • 1894- Graduates from Royal Military College
  • 1895- Father Dies young at 45
  • 1899- Churchill Campaigns for Parliament
  • 1900- Churchill wins a seat in the house of commons
  • 1908- Churchill marries Clementine Hozier
  • 1940- Churchill begins first term as Prime Minister of England
  • 1941- Churchill's stay at the White House begins
  • 1945-(February) Churchill attends the Yalta Conference
  • 1945-(July) Churchill loses the U.K. general election
  • 1946- Gives his famous "Iron Curtain" speech
  • 1951- Churchill elected Prime Minister once again
  • 1953- Wins the Nobel Prize for literature
  • 1955- Churchill retires as Prime Minister of England after his second stroke
  • 1965-(January, 24) Winston Churchill Dies at the age of 90
